Dear Colleagues,

Attached is a description of the experiment design for the planned CMIP5 experiments.  The WCRP's WGCM agreed to this design at its last meeting in September 2008.  The design should now be considered frozen, although undoubtedly there will be some mistakes found in the attached document. Please feel free to pass it on to anyone you think might have an interest.  We will let you know by email when additional information becomes available on the web.

We provide information about the following suites of CMIP5 experiments:

I   "near-term" (decadal) hindcast/prediction simulations
II  "long-term" simulations
and for especially computationally-demanding models
III "atmosphere-only" (prescribed SST) simulations.

The focus in CMIP5 is on experiments with AOGCMs (suites I and II).  This is why in the attached document, the third suite of experiments involving atmosphere-only models has not been listed separately from the "near-term" experiments.  We anticipate, however, that some NWP modeling centers and some computationally demanding model versions (e.g., with sophisticated atmospheric chemistry treatments) may only be able to perform experiments with prescribed SSTs and sea ice.  Typical models used for climate studies, however, would be expected to do experiments in suites I and/or II in addition to (or rather than) only those in suite III.

Best regards,
Ron Stouffer (Chair of the WGCM's CMIP Panel) and Karl Taylor (PCMDI)
